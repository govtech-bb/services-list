⚠️ CRITICAL SERVICE GAPS

The following essential water services are NOT found in this service registry and must be accessed directly through the relevant agencies:

  • BWA (Barbados Water Authority) - Water connections, piped water installation, brown water complaints, water infrastructure improvements
  • Water main repairs and replacements
  • New piped water connections for homes
  • Brown water issue resolution services

For water connection and supply issues, contact BWA directly. The services shown below are support services available in the government registry.

Note: This page shows only the water-related services found in the government service registry. Most core water supply services are provided by BWA and are not included in this registry.
