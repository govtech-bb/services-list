⚠️ CRITICAL SERVICE GAPS

The following essential healthcare services are NOT found in this service registry:

  • Polyclinic appointments - No service for booking appointments at polyclinics
  • Polyclinic services - No listing of services available at polyclinics (primary care, child health, etc.)
  • Hospital appointments - No online appointment booking for QEH
  • Medical records access - No service to request or view medical records
  • Laboratory/diagnostic services - No information on lab tests, X-rays, etc.
  • Specialist referrals - No system for getting specialist referrals
  • Emergency services information - No A&E wait times or emergency service details
  • Ambulance services - Not documented in registry

For medical care, contact QEH directly at 436-6450 or visit your nearest polyclinic.

Note: This page shows administrative services (birth/death registration, medical certificates) and a few basic hospital services. Most clinical healthcare services (appointments, treatments, consultations) are not in this service registry and must be accessed directly through QEH or polyclinics.
